GEANT is a pan-European research and education network dataset monitoring high-speed internet backbone traffic across European countries. The multivariate time series captures network performance metrics including link utilization, traffic volume, latency, and packet loss across a complex topology of interconnected nodes. This dataset is extensively used for network anomaly detection to identify routing issues, DDoS attacks, equipment failures, and abnormal traffic patterns. The data exhibits strong temporal dependencies with characteristic patterns reflecting European time zones, business hours, and weekend effects. Anomalies typically appear as sudden traffic surges, unexpected routing changes, link failures, or coordinated attack patterns.
